Privacy policy

A Photo a Day

Last updated: 17 May 2026

Summary

A Photo a Day is designed to work locally on your device. The app does not have internet access, does not create accounts, does not use analytics, does not show ads, and does not sell or share your data.

Data the app accesses

The app may access photos, camera input, and notification settings only for the features you choose to use.

  • Camera: used to take your daily photo.
  • Photos and gallery: used to import photos and show photos saved for this app.
  • Notifications: used only to send a daily reminder.

How data is stored

Photos remain local to your device. A Photo a Day does not upload photos or other app data to a server. Saved photos are stored through Android's local media storage so they can appear in your device gallery.

Sharing and third parties

The app does not share personal or sensitive user data with third parties. If you choose to share or export a photo or video using Android's system share sheet, that action is controlled by you and by the app you choose to share with.

Data deletion

You can delete your photos from your device's gallery or file manager. You can also remove app settings and reminders by uninstalling the app.

Children

A Photo a Day is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

A Photo a Day is developed by Sam Keshmiri. For privacy questions, use the public contact links on samkeshmiri.com.
